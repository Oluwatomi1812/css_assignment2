Why CSS so important?

CSS describes how HTML elements should be displayed. It designs your page to give it a more appealing look. But CSS doesn't just design, it also helps you to move/format elements to a desired location.

Differnce between paddings, margins and borders

  • Padding: The padding creates space around elements.
  • Border: The border is the space between the padding and margin.
  • Margin: The margin is used to generate a space around the elements.
